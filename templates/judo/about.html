About Glasgow University Judo Club

Welcome to GUJC!

At GUJC, we are passionate about judo and fostering a strong sense of community. Our club is committed to promoting the art of judo and providing a welcoming environment for enthusiasts of all skill levels.

Our Proud History

Founded in 1960, GUJC has a rich history of excellence in judo. Over the years, we have produced numerous national and international champions who started their judo journey right here.

Meet Our Instructors

Our dedicated team of certified instructors brings years of experience to the mat. They are committed to helping you develop your judo skills, whether you're a beginner or an advanced practitioner.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Train in our modern facilities equipped with top-notch mats and training equipment. We provide a safe and comfortable environment for your judo practice.

Our Achievements

Over the years, our members have achieved remarkable success in regional and national competitions. We take pride in our athletes' accomplishments and continue to strive for excellence.

Join Us Today!

Ready to embark on your judo journey with GUJC? Join us for a training session and experience the thrill of judo. We welcome new members with open arms.
